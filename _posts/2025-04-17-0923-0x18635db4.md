gM   
  
If the stars never faded,  
would I still look up?  
If the flowers never wilted,  
Would the pink blossoms in my window   
seem as bright?  
If time stretched endlessly,  
if nothing was lost,  
if every moment could be replayed…  
Would I cherish this one?
